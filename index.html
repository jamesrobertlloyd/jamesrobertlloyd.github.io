About me

I am a machine learning expert who enjoys building end to end systems. I am currently leading the development of the natural language processing (NLP) and anomaly detection product verticals at Abacus AI. Before Abacus, I was a technical co-founder at Qlearsite and I am currently their AI advisor.

I completed my PhD at the Machine Learning Group, University of Cambridge working with Zoubin Ghahramani. My research ultimately focused on the automation of machine learning model construction and the rational allocation of computational resources when building models.

Before my PhD I was a management consultant at the Boston Consulting Group and before that I received a B.A. in mathematics and M.Phil in Statistics from the University of Cambridge.
